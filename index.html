Personal Information

My name is Nikolaos Lesgidis and I have recently finished my PhD at the Civil Engineering Department of Aristotle University, Thessaloniki, Greece. My research interests include but are not limited to Computational aspects of Structural mechanics, Risk Assesment of Bridge structures, multibody railway dynamic simulation and design optimization.
Beyond my research oriented work, I have worked in the industry field of software development, succesfully completing multiple projects in the field of Finite element simulation for geotechnical Engineering.
