Personal Information

My name is Nikolaos Lesgidis and I have recently finished my PhD at the Civil Engineering Department of Aristotle University, Thessaloniki, Greece. My work is focused in the fields of computational structural mechanics, Risk Assesment of Bridge structures, multibody railway dynamic simulation and design optimization. I have created this website to be used as a code and software sharing platform,thus you will find a number of scripts complementary to my published work in reduced SSI simulation, along with the release executables of any freely distributed software i have solely or co-developed in the past.
